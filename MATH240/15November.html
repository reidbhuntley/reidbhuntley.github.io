Norm

For any inner product, there is an associated notion of a norm (the magnitude of a vector): ∥v∥=⟨v,v⟩\lVert v\rVert=\sqrt{\langle v,v\rangle}∥v∥=⟨v,v⟩​.

For Rn\R^nRn,
⟨v,v⟩=v12+...+vn2∥v∥=v12+...+vn2\begin{aligned} \langle v,v\rangle&=v_1^2+...+v_n^2\\ \lVert v\rVert&=\sqrt{v_1^2+...+v_n^2} \end{aligned}⟨v,v⟩∥v∥​=v12​+...+vn2​=v12​+...+vn2​​​
